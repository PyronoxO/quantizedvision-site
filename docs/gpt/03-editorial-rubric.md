# Editorial Rubric (Score 1-5)

## 1) Accuracy
- No fabricated facts.
- Claims align with source.

## 2) Originality
- Wording is clearly rewritten.
- No copycat phrasing.

## 3) Clarity
- Main point is immediately understandable.
- No jargon overload.

## 4) Reader Interest
- Strong hook.
- Clear "why it matters."

## 5) Brand Fit
- Matches Quantized Vision tone.
- Confident, modern, non-generic.

## Pass Threshold
- Minimum 4/5 on Accuracy and Originality.
- Overall average 4.0+ for publish-ready drafts.
