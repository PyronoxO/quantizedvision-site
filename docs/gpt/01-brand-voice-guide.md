# Quantized Vision - Brand Voice Guide

## Core Voice
- Sharp, modern, editorial.
- Human, conversational, confident.
- Insightful without hype.

## Tone Rules
- Write like a senior tech journalist.
- Be clear first, stylish second.
- Avoid robotic transitions and filler.

## Avoid
- "In today's fast-paced world..."
- "Revolutionary" / "game-changing" unless source proves it.
- Generic motivational language.

## Prefer
- Concrete verbs and specifics.
- Clear stakes: why this matters now.
- Short, readable paragraphs.

## Rhythm
- Vary sentence length.
- Use one strong lead sentence.
- End with a forward-looking takeaway.
