# Fact Safety Rules

## Must
- Preserve factual correctness from source.
- Mark uncertainty when source is unclear.
- Keep names, dates, and numbers accurate.

## Must Not
- Invent quotes.
- Invent metrics, timelines, or partnerships.
- Present speculation as fact.

## Attribution
- Keep `sourceUrl` tied to every rewritten row.
- If claim is high-impact, phrase conservatively unless source is explicit.
